Project Overview

Plus, EveryBody Counts is an AI-powered healthcare management system designed to help seniors monitor their health conditions and receive customized care.

Problem Statement


Elderly people (75+) need tailored help to use online healthcare platforms for appointments and tracking their health because services moved online and they have low tech skills.


User Personas


User Journey Map


Our Solution


Voice Assistant

Always-on voice assistant to help complete tasks.

Anomaly Detection and Prevention

Monitor and control your health condition with easy-to-read graphs. Receive advice and support from Plus.

OCR Text Detection

Designed to aid with vision problems, Text Detection lets users quickly scan packaging and medical prescriptions. It also allows for fast addition of reminders, simplifying medication and task management.

Recommendation System

Access medical and healthcare facilities effortlessly, book and manage health reports with Plus, receive reminders for activities and medication, and get suggestions for a healthier lifestyle to enhance well-being.
