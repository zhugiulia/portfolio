Zhulandia

Banknote Design
Time 2021
Course ABABO - Graphic Design Course
Supervisors Danilo Danisi

Project Overview

This is a personal project that I made for the final exam of my graphic design course. My idea was to design a series of banknotes for my inner country, called “ZHULANDIA”. My favorite philosophers, writers, artists, animals, and plants are reproduced on each banknote.

Moodboard

Sketches

For each banknote, I made three sets of drawings: main characters of my country, (with the appearance of an animal, including me as an ostrich, Dante Alighieri as a blobfish, A. Schopenhauer as a goat, my teacher as a bear, and lastly V. van Gogh as a cat), a second set for my favorite plants, and the last one for some of my best-loved animals.
